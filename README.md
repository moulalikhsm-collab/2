EcoFriend – AI Smart Plantation Assistant

📖 Overview

EcoFriend is an AI-powered smart plantation assistant designed to help students, home gardeners, and plant enthusiasts grow healthy plants with confidence.

The application leverages Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Environmental Analytics to provide personalized plant recommendations, disease detection, growth forecasting, and smart plant care guidance.
